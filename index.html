Welcome to the Debian Games Team homepage!

The purpose of the Debian Games Team is to coordinate, share common problems and solutions and to maintain games collaboratively.

Featured Game

Slune

Slune is a 3D game. It includes racing, car-crashing, and lots of action. In addition, a multiplayer mode is available.

Slune is a fantasy futuristic 3D racing game with "Tux" the penguin.
The goal of the game is to deliver pharmaceuticals to Africa and to escape from an evil shark and its supporters.
To win the game, the player must achieve missions in 15 levels. Slune does not only support a single player mode, but it is also possible to play against others over a local network connection.

# apt-get install slune

Introductory Information (or the path to debianhood)

If you want to join the group you need to register an account on alioth and ask for being added to the pkg-games group, preferrably by sending a short introduction mail to debian-devel-games@lists.debian.org.
